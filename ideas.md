<idea>
  **Design Movement**: Cyber-Industrial / Neo-Brutalist
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Raw Functionality**: Exposed structural elements, monospaced fonts for data, high contrast.
  2. **Security First**: Visual language of encryption, shields, and secure terminals.
  3. **Precision Engineering**: Grid lines, technical schematics, and blueprint aesthetics.
  4. **Stealth & Anonymity**: Dark mode default, "ghost" elements, subtle glitched effects.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  - **Primary**: Deep Charcoal / Void Black (#0a0a0a) - Represents the hidden web.
  - **Accent**: Electric Cyan (#00f0ff) or Neon Green (#00ff41) - Signals active security/encryption.
  - **Secondary**: Slate Grey (#64748b) - For structural elements and secondary text.
  - **Intent**: Evoke the feeling of a high-tech command center or a hacker's terminal. Trust through technical superiority.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - **Asymmetric Grid**: Broken grids with overlapping elements to suggest complexity and depth.
  - **Terminal Windows**: Content sections framed like terminal windows or HUD displays.
  - **Data Streams**: Background elements resembling scrolling code or data packets (subtle).

  **Signature Elements**:
  - **Glitch Text**: Headlines that occasionally "glitch" to reveal the "IdentityForge" name.
  - **Shield/Lock Icons**: Custom 3D-rendered glass shields or padlocks.
  - **Fingerprint Motifs**: Abstract fingerprint patterns used as background textures or section dividers.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - **Hover Reveals**: Elements reveal more data or "decrypt" on hover.
  - **Typing Effects**: Text appears as if being typed in real-time.
  - **Smooth Scroll with Parallax**: Sections slide over each other like blast doors.

  **Animation**:
  - **Entrance**: Elements slide in with a "snap" effect, like mechanical parts locking into place.
  - **Background**: subtle, slow-moving network graph or matrix rain (very subtle).

  **Typography System**:
  - **Headings**: **JetBrains Mono** or **Space Mono** (Monospaced, technical).
  - **Body**: **Inter** or **Roboto** (Clean, readable sans-serif for long text).
  - **Hierarchy**: Large, bold monospaced headers > Technical labels (uppercase, small) > Readable body text.
</idea>

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - **Floating Cards**: Content contained in frosted glass cards floating over a dynamic background.
  - **Central Focus**: Key value propositions centered with ample breathing room.
  - **Soft Boundaries**: Sections blend into each other with gradients rather than hard lines.

  **Signature Elements**:
  - **Glass Cards**: Frosted background with thin, light borders.

  - **Smooth Gradients**: Backgrounds that slowly shift colors.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:

  **Design Movement**: Swiss Style / International Typographic Style (Dark Mode)
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Grid Systems**: Strict, mathematical grids for organizing information.
  2. **Objective Photography/Graphics**: High-quality, realistic device mockups and interface screenshots.
  3. **Typography as Image**: Large, bold typography used as the primary visual element.
  4. **Clarity & Readability**: Content is king; design serves to make it clear.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  - **Primary**: Black (#000000) - Stark, bold background.
  - **Accent**: International Orange (#ff3b30) or Signal Blue (#007aff) - High contrast for calls to action.
  - **Secondary**: White (#ffffff) - For text and lines.
  - **Intent**: Communicate efficiency, reliability, and professional grade. "The tool for pros."

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - **Modular Grid**: Content organized into clear, rectangular modules.
  - **Split Screen**: 50/50 layouts comparing "Before" (tracked) and "After" (IdentityForge).
  - **Horizontal Scroll**: Sections that scroll horizontally to reveal features.

  **Signature Elements**:
  - **Bold Lines**: Thick dividing lines between sections.
  - **Huge Type**: Headlines that span the entire width of the screen.
  - **Minimal Icons**: Simple, stroke-based icons.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - **Snap Scroll**: Scrolling snaps to sections for a deliberate reading experience.
  - **Cursor Effects**: Custom cursor that inverts colors or expands over clickable elements.

  **Animation**:
  - **Slide Up**: Text and images slide up from behind invisible masks.
  - **Staggered Reveal**: List items appear one by one.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Headings**: **Helvetica Now** or **Neue Haas Grotesk** (The classic Swiss font).
  - **Body**: **Inter** (Neutral, legible).
  - **Hierarchy**: Massive headers > Bold subheaders > Regular body text.
</idea>
